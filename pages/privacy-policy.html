Privacy Policy Details

Information We Collect

We may collect information when you contact us, sign up for newsletters, request a consultation, or access our services. This may include your name, email address, phone number, company name, and information related to your SharePoint environment.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide consulting and optimization services for SharePoint environments.
  • Respond to inquiries, requests, and technical support questions.
  • Send updates, newsletters, or communications relevant to our services (with your consent).
  • Analyze website usage to improve content and service delivery (all analytics are anonymized).

Client Data Handling

During consulting engagements, we may access your SharePoint sites and related enterprise systems. All client data accessed during audits, configuration reviews, or optimizations is treated as confidential and will not be shared with third parties without explicit consent.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website does not use cookies to collect personal information. We may use minimal analytics (e.g., visitor count) for performance and usability improvements, but all such data is anonymized.

Third-Party Tools

If third-party tools or integrations (e.g., Power Automate, Azure Functions) are used during consulting, any data transmitted is limited to what is necessary to provide the service. We do not store or distribute this data outside the scope of the engagement.

Data Retention

Client records, reports, and email communications are retained only as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, or for audit purposes. Once no longer required, data is securely deleted.

External Links

Our website may link to external sites. Speed Up SharePoint is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party websites.

Legal Compliance

We comply with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR and CCPA, when handling personal data of clients and visitors. You may contact us for requests related to your personal data.
