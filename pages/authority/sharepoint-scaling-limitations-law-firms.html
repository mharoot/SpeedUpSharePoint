Why Law Firms Hit SharePoint Limits Early

Law firms generate exceptionally high document volumes through litigation, discovery, transactional work, and regulatory retention requirements. SharePoint environments in legal organizations often scale faster than originally planned.

Common Legal-Specific Pressure Points

  • Case-centric libraries growing into millions of files
  • Frequent security changes driven by matter teams
  • Heavy metadata usage for classification and retention
  • Discovery searches across historical matters

Security Trimming at Scale

Legal environments rely heavily on item-level permissions. At scale, permission trimming becomes a dominant performance cost during search, navigation, and bulk operations.

Architectural Reality

SharePoint works best as a governed document authority. High-volume discovery, analytics, and reporting workloads require complementary systems designed for scale.
